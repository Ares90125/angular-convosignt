How it works

Step 1 - Signup

In this step, you authorize Convosight to get you profile picture, name and email id (depending on your privacy setting) from Facebook.

Step 2 - Add Group to Convosight

You authorize Convosight to get basic group details of groups you admin.

Step 3 - Add Convosight to your group

You add Convosight app on your group from Facebook group settings page.

Step 4 - Convosight Analyze the data

Once all of the above steps are done, Convosight pulls in all the conversation to analyze them and find some meaningful and actionable insight. It uses Data Science and Machine Learning to make smart recommendations.

Note: Convosight is a Facebook Approved app. It uses only FB APIs to exchange data. It does not share data with anyone else than the group admins and authorized moderators added by Admin on Convosight
